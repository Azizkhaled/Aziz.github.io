Hello and welcome to my corner of the internet!

I earned my B.Sc. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Middle East Technical University, Ankara, Turkey, back in 2021. Building on that foundation, I pursued my passion at Carleton University, Ottawa, Canada, where I achieved an M.Eng. degree in electrical and computer engineering in 2023, specializing in data science.

Currently, I am honored to serve as a Research Associate with the Multimedia Research Group at Carleton University, a role I've held since 2022. Throughout my academic and professional voyage, I have been deeply immersed in the world of machine learning, consistently driven to tackle intricate challenges within computer vision, signal processing, and natural language processing.

While my academic journey has provided me with invaluable knowledge, I am also humbled by the opportunities I've had to collaborate with brilliant minds in the field. My research pursuits reflect my unwavering enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of machine learning and delving into the depths of deep learning.

As I continue to explore these captivating domains, my goal is to contribute meaningfully to the advancement of technology, seeking solutions that resonate with real-world needs. This portfolio is my avenue for sharing my projects, thoughts, and insights. Just like the ever-evolving nature of technology, I intend to keep updating this space with my latest ventures, ensuring that the enthusiasm I have for my work shines through.
